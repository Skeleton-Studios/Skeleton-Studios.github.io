#3: THE DELTA PERSPECTIVE CHALLENGES CHALLENGE

03-14-2025

by: James

Hey, you! Do you want to play Skeleton Studios' next video game for free? Take the challenges challenge for a chance to do just that! Delta Perspective is receiving a new build, featuring QoL changes, rebalances, and the all new challenges system.

CHALLENGES

If you complete the demo level without ever activating a checkpoint, you will be shown a set of challenges. They'll be highlighted in gold if you completed them during this run.

THE GIVEAWAY

For every player who submits a video of them playing through Delta Perspective and completing at least one challenge, they will be entered into a giveaway. Once the game releases, at least two random names will be chosen, and those chosen will recieve a free copy of the game!

RULES

For every one of the challenges you complete, you will receive an entry into the raffle pool. So if you've completed five challenges, you will receive five entries. One of the challenges listed is called "Through a Needle's Eye", and is rewarded to those who complete every other challenge. If you manage to do so in your run, you will be entered into a seperate raffle pool. So if you want better odds, you've really gotta work for it!

SUBMITTING

Record a run of yourself playing through the new Delta Perspective demo build, and upload it to Youtube. Ensure that this footage is uncut, that it starts before you hit the new game button, and that it ends after the game shows which challenges you've unlocked. We encourage you to include voiceover or facecam. Once it's uploaded, be sure to title the video with the name "Delta Perspective" so we can find it. If you want to ensure we collect your submission, please send it to us via any of our communication channels (Twitter, Discord, etc).
